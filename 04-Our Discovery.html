Our Discovery

Welcome to Our Discovery Section

In this part of our website, we present an interactive Virtual Laboratory designed to deepen your understanding of Large Language Models (LLMs). Users can explore how different configurations of LLM parameters—such as the number of layers and the size of the training data—impact model performance. This hands-on experience serves to illuminate the intricate architecture of LLMs and the trade-offs involved in their training processes, providing a clearer comprehension of these powerful tools.
